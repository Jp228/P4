The field

Just like the baseball field, Cricket field is some what similar. The cricket field is oval. There is a meaning behind this set up. If the ball is rolling on the ground and touches the line of the oval, it is considered as four run/scores. If the ball goes above the stadium and passes the oval, it is considered as six runs/scores. The yellow rectangle is where the player plays, bowler and batter. Rest of the players are scattered in the field. The stumps are placed behind the batsman. Behind the stump there is a player kind of like a goal keeper just like how in baseball.
